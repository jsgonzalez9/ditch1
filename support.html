Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Ditch?

Download Ditch from the App Store, create an account with your email, and complete the onboarding process. You'll provide information about your vaping habits and goals, which helps us personalize your experience. Then start tracking your progress immediately!

Is Ditch free to use?

Yes! Ditch offers a free version with core features including basic tracking, statistics, and community access. Premium subscriptions unlock advanced features like detailed health timelines, craving management tools, buddy system, and more. Premium costs $2.99/month or $25.00/year.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Ditch"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Can I request a refund?

Refunds are handled directly by Apple through their refund policy. To request a refund:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your Ditch subscription purchase
  4. Select "Report a Problem"
  5. Follow the prompts to request a refund
How accurate are the health timelines?

Health timelines are based on peer-reviewed scientific research about the body's recovery after quitting nicotine and vaping. However, everyone's body is different, and your actual recovery may vary based on factors like age, overall health, duration of vaping, and individual physiology. The timelines are meant to be motivational guides, not personalized medical advice.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! We take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely using industry-standard practices. We never sell your personal information. Community posts can be made anonymously if you prefer. For detailed information, please review our Privacy Policy.

Can I use Ditch on multiple devices?

Yes! Your account syncs across all your devices where you're signed in with the same email. Your progress, statistics, and settings will be kept in sync automatically as long as you have an internet connection.

What if I relapse or have a setback?

Setbacks are a normal part of the quitting journey! Ditch is designed to support you through ups and downs. If you vape after being quit, simply log it and keep going. Your historical data is preserved, and you can set new goals. Remember, progress isn't always linear, and every day you reduce is still progress. Our community and buddy system are here to support you through difficult times.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Ditch app
  2. Go to Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll to "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

Does Ditch replace medical treatment?

No. Ditch is a tracking and support tool, not a medical device or treatment. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you're experiencing nicotine withdrawal symptoms or health issues related to vaping, please consult with a healthcare professional. Consider combining Ditch with professional medical support for the best results.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Premium users can export their tracking data, statistics, and progress reports. This feature is available in the Profile settings. Your data will be provided in a standard format (CSV or PDF) that you can save or share with healthcare providers.

How does the buddy system work?

The buddy system pairs you with other users who are also working to quit vaping. You can send messages, check in on each other's progress, share encouragement, and hold each other accountable. All buddy connections require mutual consent, and you can manage your buddy list at any time. This feature is available to Premium users.

What platforms is Ditch available on?

Ditch is currently available on iOS devices (iPhone and iPad) through the Apple App Store. We're working on bringing Ditch to more platforms in the future. Stay tuned for updates!
